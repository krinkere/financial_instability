ADX is used to quantify trend strength. ADX calculations are based on a moving average of price range expansion over a given period of time (default is 14) ADX is plotted as a single line that registers trend strength whether price is trending up or down. The indicator is usually plotted in the same window as the two directional movement indicator (DMI) lines, from which ADX is derived. When the +DMI(PDI) is above the -DMI(NDI), prices are moving up, and ADX measures the strength of the uptrend.
